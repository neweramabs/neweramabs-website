new/era/mabs revolutionizes antibody developments for all means of applications in the research, diagnostic and therapeutic space.

At new/era/mabs, we leverage advanced deep-tech selection platforms to deliver high-throughput antibody services that accelerate your research and development endeavors. Our proprietary platform offers an unparalleled advantage with iterative and indefinite screening of antibody-secreting cells. With flexible options for checking cross-reactivities, affinities, and different isotypes, we ensure precision and accuracy at every step of the process. In combination with our specific VHH antibody generating workflow we can create unique tools to support every project.

Our mission

We are on a mission to provide sophisticated platform solutions to simplify and accelerate antibody discovery workflows for every needed application. With these solutions and tools we enable and support scientists to develop significant applications and treatments for personalized health care.

Our history

Founded as an university spin off in 2014, new/era/mabs is dedicated to translate basic research into new technologies and create solutions for supporting the development of novel diagnostic tests and therapeutic treatments. Starting in 2008 as an academic group, the team created single applications to bridge time-consuming and laborious steps during immunization and selection of antibody-secreting cells. Till 2014, the single applications were merged to an all-round platform approach, followed by filing the first patent for selma - our highly flexible selection approach based on novel transgenic fusion cell lines. Since then, the approach is constantly evolving and supplemented with new applications to provide the highest usability standards. Now, the system allows iterative and indefinite screenings of fusion pools against any given target including cross-reactivity, desired affinities and different antibody isotypes. Our journey culminates in creating a novel and proprietary antibody selection platform starting from scratch, bringing this to a professional and commercial level to offer this as a customized solution to every scientist. This is our contribution to the world for a better development of patient specific treatments.
